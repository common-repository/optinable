== Installation ==

### Automatic installation

1. Search for OptinAble through 'Plugins > Add New' interface.
2. Find the plugin box of OptinAble  and click on the 'Install Now' button.
3. Then activate the OptinAble plugin.
4. Create a new Campaign: After installing the plugin, a new menu called "OptinAble" will appear. Visit that page and click the + icon to Add New Popup.

### Manual installation

1. Upload the optinable folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the OptinAble plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to the OptinAble settings page to configure your opt-in forms and customize their appearance.
4. Create and publish your opt-in forms using the provided shortcode or widget.
5. Start capturing leads and growing your email list with OptinAble!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I customize the design of my OptinAble Popups? =

Yes, OptinAble provides customizable templates that you can easily modify to match your website's design and branding. You can customize colors, fonts, images, and more to create a cohesive look for your opt-in forms.

= Can I target specific audiences with my OptinAble forms? =

Absolutely! OptinAble offers advanced targeting options that allow you to display your opt-in forms to specific audiences based on various criteria such as page type, referral source, location, and more. This helps you tailor your messages and offers to specific segments of your audience.

= Do I need coding skills to use this plugin? =

Absolutely not, You can use free available Templates in the OptinAble plugin and configure those in just few clicks.

= Is OptinAble mobile responsive? =

Yes, OptinAble ensures that your opt-in forms are fully responsive and mobile-friendly. They will adapt to different screen sizes and display correctly on all devices, providing a seamless user experience.
